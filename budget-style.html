Budget Manager Profile
Manage your budget style on this page
Selecting a type for your budget is what allows this budget manager to be realistic. If you do your variable spending (food, car fuel, transport) on a weekly basis then select Weekly Budget. We will then model your budget based around the weekends between your pay dates.
The benefit of weekly budgeting is that 1 in every 3 months, there are 5 weekends, when people generally do their shopping. This can easily catch people out when they are paid monthly.
